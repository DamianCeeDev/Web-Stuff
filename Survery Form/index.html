Are you Human or AI?

Welcome to our survey, "Are You Human or AI?" This survey aims to explore the understanding and perception of artificial intelligence (AI) among participants. Whether you're a human curious about AI or an AI entity engaging with human experiences, your insights are valuable to us. Through a series of thought-provoking questions, we seek to understand how individuals perceive the capabilities, limitations, ethical considerations, and societal impacts of AI. Your responses will contribute to broader discussions surrounding the integration of AI into our daily lives and its implications for the future. Your participation is anonymous and greatly appreciated. By sharing your perspectives, you're helping us gain valuable insights into the evolving relationship between humans and AI. Thank you for taking the time to contribute to our research.
